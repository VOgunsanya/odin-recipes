Description

Ingredients

  • Brown sugar
  • Old fashioned oats
  • Vanilla extract
  • Heavy cream
  • Butter
  • Peaches
  • Cinnamon

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  2. Mix oats, brown sugar, vanilla extract, and heavy cream
  3. Place peaches into backing dish with brown sugar, a diced stick of butter, and a bit of vanilla extract and cinnamon
  4. Dollop oat mix onto peaches
  5. Bake in the oven for 45 minutes and enjoy
