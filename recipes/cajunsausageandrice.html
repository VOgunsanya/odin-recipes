Description

Ingredients

  • Long grain rice
  • Bell pepper
  • Andouille sausage
  • Beef broth
  • Italian seasoning
  • Sun roasted tomatoes

Steps

  1. Decide whether to cook the rice in the skillet or separately in a rice cooker
  2. Brown the sausage on the skillet
  3. Deglaze with beef broth and the chopped bell pepper and sun roasted tomatoes
  4. Mix with the rice and serve
