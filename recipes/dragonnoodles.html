Pork and Peanut Dragon Noodles

Description

Ingredients

  • Egg noodles
  • Chili Garlic Sauce
  • Ground Pork
  • Chopped Peanuts
  • Soy Sauce
  • Brown Sugar

Steps

  1. Mix together the chili garlic sauce, brown sugar, and soy sauce
  2. Begin boiling the water for the noodles as you brown the pork
  3. As the pork finished browning, add the peanuts and dragon sauce mixture
  4. Finish by adding the noodles into the pan, and serve.
